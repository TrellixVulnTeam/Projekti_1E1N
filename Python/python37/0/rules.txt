Navigation through the main menu - arrow keys to select the option and space to confirm 
(DO NOT USE RETURN BUTTON (the left enter) FOR ANYTHING)
Solo play - player 1 moves on arrows and shoots on enter key (the right one)
Multiplayer - player 1 is the same, player 2 moves on wasd and shoots on space key
When inside game if you want to go back to the main menu press escape button

-Shoot down robots for points
-Don't touch the walls - they are electric :)
-When Otto the Enemy spawns avoid him at all costs
-Even rarely the star will appear, collect it for extra life
-You can leave the level whenever you want without any consequences 
-When all of the players lose all lives the game ends, the player with the most points goes to the highscore

ENJOY :)
